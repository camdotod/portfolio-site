My Name is Camryn O'Donnell

I am a multidisciplinary designer with an interest in UX/UI design & design research.

I'm passionate about design and I'm always looking for new ways in which design can be used as a tool to bridge gaps and help others! Design research is one of my areas of expertise and I am eager to explore a wide range of fields and design methodologies including co-design, participatory design, and speculative design.

What interests me most about design is learning how people interact with the objects and systems surrounding them and I think this context is key to uncovering the root of any problem. Through continuous collaboration and learning I hope to make a positive impact on the world around me.

When I’m not working, I love being creative, whether that’s drawing, sewing, making music, or cooking. I also enjoy playing video games & tabletop RPGs with friends!

Tags:

Design Research

Product Design

.
expand_more

Objective

To investigate if alternative forms of work, such as third spaces, could benefit delivery of psychotherapy and counselling in Ontario.

Summary

Using design research methods, clients & therapists were interviewed to reveal core problem areas within Ontario’s mental health care system. With these perspectives in mind, a product was generated to make real the possibility of a new system for the practice of therapy.

Objective

To create a mobile app that improves the Ottawa Public Transit experience.

Summary

With the help of team members, design research was conducted into the user experience of Ottawa transit riders. Participants’ experiences motivated the development of a transit app prototype, which was mocked up in Figma.
