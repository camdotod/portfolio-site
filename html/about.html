About Me

I am a multidisciplinary designer with an interest in UX/UI design & design research.

I studied at Carleton University in Ottawa, Ontario and obtained a Bachelor of Industrial Design in 2023.I have experience working in commercial product design and in community projects.

I'm passionate about design and I'm always looking for new ways in which design can be used as a tool to bridge gaps and help others! Design research is one of my areas of expertise and I am eager to explore a wide range of fields and design methodologies including co-design, participatory design, and speculative design. What interests me most about design is learning how people interact with the objects and systems surrounding them and I think this context is key to uncovering the root of any problem. Through continuous collaboration and learning I hope to make a positive impact on the world around me.

What is a multidisciplinary designer?

A multidisciplinary designer is a designer who has access to a broad skill set encompassing multiple fields including industrial/product design, UX/UI design, graphic design, interaction design, and web design. For me, the title multidisciplinary designer reflects my belief that sometimes we must think beyond the traditional form of a solution and represents my commitment to continuous learning.

When I’m not working, I love being creative, whether that’s drawing, sewing, making music, or cooking. I also enjoy playing video games & tabletop RPGs with friends!
